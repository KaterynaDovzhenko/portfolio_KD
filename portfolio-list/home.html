HomeLab

For HomeLab, I crafted a captivating hero section that invites users to step into the role of architect for their dream home. The design opens with an inspiring message: "Create your dream house," drawing users into an interactive experience where they can design and customize every detail. This hero section sets the stage for creativity and personalization, reflecting HomeLab's unique offering—a virtual space for users to explore their style and bring their ideal home to life. The result is an engaging introduction that makes users feel empowered to start building from the very first moment.
