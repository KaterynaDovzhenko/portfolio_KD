Lum

For Lum, I designed an impactful hero section that draws users into the heart of creative storytelling. The design introduces users with an inviting call to action: "Start your own story – Welcome to the world of creative storytelling!" Paired with visually captivating elements, the hero section sets the stage for an inspiring experience, highlighting Lum's easy-to-use tools and vibrant community. This section captures the essence of creativity, inviting users to begin crafting and sharing their unique narratives from the very first glance.
