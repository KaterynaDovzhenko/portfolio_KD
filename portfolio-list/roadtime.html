Road Time

For Road Time, I had the pleasure of designing the logo and creating a visually engaging website that connects customers with their next set of wheels. This project aimed to deliver a compelling user interface (UI) that captures the thrill of car shopping while ensuring a smooth user experience (UX). The design prominently features inviting prompts like, "Looking for your next set of wheels? Look no further than our collection of cars for sale!" Each page was thoughtfully crafted to showcase an extensive range of vehicles, making it easy for users to explore their options. The result is a responsive, user-friendly website that transforms the car-buying journey into an exciting and enjoyable experience.
